Lasagna Recipe

Image by timolina on Freepik

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with savory fillings. Traditional versions feature rich meat sauce (like Bolognese), creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked together to create a hearty, comforting meal. The top layer is typically golden and bubbly, forming a flavorful crust.

Lasagna is known for its deliciously layered texture and bold, satisfying flavors, making it a favorite comfort food around the world. Variations may include vegetables, different meats, or alternative sauces, but the layered format remains iconic.

Ingredients

  • Meat :This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic :An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products :You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar :Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasoning :his lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles :Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses :Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg : An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
    • Heat a pan over medium heat.
    • Cook the diced onion and garlic until softened.
    • Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
    • Stir in marinara sauce and season with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs.
    • Simmer for 10–15 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Cook the Lasagna noodles

    3. (Skip if using over-ready noodles)

    • Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente.
    • Drain and lay flat on a towel to prevent sticking.
  3. Make the Ricotta Mixture
    • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, a pinch of salt, and some chopped parsley (optional).
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
    • Add a layer of noodles.
    • Spread ricotta mixture over noodles.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella and a little Parmesan.
    • Repeat layers: sauce, noodles, ricotta, cheese.
    • Finish with a final layer of noodles, sauce, and lots of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  5. Bake
    • Cover with foil (you can spray it with oil to prevent sticking).
    • Bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake another 15–20 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  6. Rest and Serve
    • Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. This helps it set and makes serving easier.
    • Garnish with chopped parsley or basil if desired.
