Sweet and Sour Chicken

Description

Sweet and Sour Chicken is a popular Chinese-American dish known for its delicious balance of flavors. The chicken is typically battered and deep-fried until crispy, then tossed in a tangy, vibrant sauce that combines the sweetness of sugar with the tartness of vinegar and ketchup. The sauce is often enhanced with ingredients like soy sauce, garlic, and pineapple, giving it a fresh, fruity taste.

The dish is usually served with bell peppers, onions, and sometimes pineapple chunks, which add extra texture and flavor to the dish. The result is a perfect mix of savory, sweet, and sour tastes thatâ€™s both comforting and exciting to the palate. Sweet and sour chicken is often enjoyed with steamed rice and is a staple on Chinese takeout menus.

Ingredients

  • For the sauce :water, pineapple juice from a can, white sugar, white vinegar, and orange food coloring
  • For the batter :cornstarch, self-rising flour, vegetable oil, an egg, salt, and white pepper
  • Chicken :Cut eight boneless, skinless chicken breast halves into 1-inch cubes for this sweet and sour chicken recipe.
  • Pine apple and Green pepper :Pineapple chunks and two green bell peppers (cut into 1-inch pieces) give the dish color and flavor.

Steps

  1. Boil 1 and 1/2 cups of water with pineapple juice, sugar, vinegar, and orange food coloring.
  2. Make a cornstarch slurry, then mix it into the sauce until thickened.
  3. Make the batter, then stir in the chicken pieces until well-coated.
  4. Fry the chicken.
  5. Layer the peppers, pineapple, and chicken on a platter.
  6. Pour the sauce over the top.
